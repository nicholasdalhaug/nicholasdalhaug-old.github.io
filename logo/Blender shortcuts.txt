Blender shortcuts:


PANELS

Transform panel: +sign up in the right corner. This panel includes the setting of the background. Useful using top view and orthographic view. 



MODES

Edit mode: Tab. 



HOTKEYS

Top view: Numpad 7
Orthographic view: Numpad 5
Transform: g. Enables moving the selected vertices. Can also hold down the right mouse button. 
Extrude: e. Can append vertices. 
Fill: f. 
Cirlce select: c. 

The numpad generally handles views.
